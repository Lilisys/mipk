Michigan Parkour

Michigan Parkour is a family/community - not a sports club. We do not compete or perform. Our goals vary but mostly people do parkour to get to know themselves and their nature better, get stronger physically and mentally, meet new friends and help each other progress in many ways. Remember: physical movement is only the surface of parkour, we “Move mental” as well.

FAQ's

  • When and where do you meet?
    • We meet every Tuesday and Thursday at 5:00 pm and Sunday at 4:00 pm at the School of Dentistry courtyard.
  • How much experience do I need to participate?
    • None, Beginners are welcome!
  • Do I need to be a student?
    • Nope! We welcome everyone, regardless of age, gender, race, etc.
  • Does it cost money to join?
    • Nope!
  • What if I can't do splits/flips/one-armed-pullups?
    • Neither can most of us! That's why we train ;)
